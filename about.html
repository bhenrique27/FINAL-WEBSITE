About Me

Hey, thanks for stopping by my little corner of the internet. This site is basically my “professional starter kit” — the place where I’m slowly putting together everything I’m learning in school, the skills I’m building, and the things I actually care about. I’m a Communications major at UNM, and one thing my classes constantly push is how important it is to create a digital presence that feels real, not like a cookie-cutter template. So that’s what this site is about — me showing who I am, in my own voice, while learning the technical side of web development at the same time.

When I’m not studying or working, I’m usually gaming, hanging out with my girlfriend, watching movies, or learning something new. I like breaking things down, figuring out how they work, and then putting my own spin on them — which is probably why web design has grown on me. This site is definitely a work in progress, but it represents where I’m at right now and where I’m headed. As I keep learning HTML and CSS, this page will evolve with me.
