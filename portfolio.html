My Portfolio

Welcome to the portfolio section of my site — basically the place where I keep track of the work I’ve done, the skills I'm building, and the projects that show who I am as a student and as a creator. Since I’m majoring in Communications at UNM, a lot of what I work on connects back to messaging, storytelling, design, and how people interact with content online. This page gives you a quick look into the things I’m learning and how they’re helping shape what I want to do in the future.

Some of the projects I’ve enjoyed the most include class assignments where I got to mix creativity with technical skills — things like written pieces, digital media work, and even basic web development. Every project feels like a small step toward becoming more confident in what I can do professionally. Even though this site is still pretty simple, it represents proof that I’m learning new tools and pushing myself out of my comfort zone.

As I grow in my major and gain more experience, I’ll keep adding work here. My goal is that one day this page becomes a full collection of everything I’ve created — school projects, writing samples, media pieces, and real-world work too. Thanks for taking the time to check it out, and feel free to come back as this page evolves.
